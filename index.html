Business English 1

In this module, you will be taught to communicate in a concise and formal business style in the form of writing various business reports and doing a presentation in Business English. Furthermore, you will learn how to quickly and accurately understand and use information through using refined reading strategies.

Co-Creative Entrepreneurship 1

The Co-Creative Entrepreneurship (CCE) project is devided in two modules: CCE1 in block 3 and CCE2 in block 4. The mission of the CCE project is twofold. Firstly it aims at creating awareness among students of what it takes to successfully start and build a company from scratch and of how the various elements (tools, competencies, disciplines, teamwork) in business development and management fit together. Secondly to develop entrepreneurial and other employability skills.
